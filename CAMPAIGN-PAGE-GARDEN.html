Story

In many urban neighborhoods, families struggle with limited access to fresh produce, rising food prices, and the absence of green spaces. Empty lots sit unused while communities continue to rely on expensive vegetables from faraway markets. Over time, this affects not just nutrition, but overall well-being.

This Community Garden Project will transform a vacant lot into a thriving green space where residents can grow vegetables, learn sustainable gardening, and come together as a neighborhood. Your support helps turn bare soil into opportunity, nourishment, and community pride.

What is the campaign about?

Establish a functional community garden where residents can plant, grow, and harvest vegetables, plus host workshops on sustainability and urban farming.
