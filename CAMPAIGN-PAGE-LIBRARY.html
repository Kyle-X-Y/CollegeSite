Story

For years, the local library has been a quiet refuge for students, families, and anyone seeking a place to learn. But as time passed, the building became worn down—broken shelves, outdated lighting, and limited seating slowly made it harder for people to study comfortably. What was once a vibrant learning space now struggles to meet the needs of a growing community that depends on it.

This renovation campaign aims to bring life back into the library. By upgrading the interiors, adding new shelves, improving lighting, and creating better reading spaces, the goal is to rebuild an environment where knowledge, creativity, and curiosity can thrive again.

What is the campaign about?

This campaign seeks to transform the old library into a modern, welcoming community learning center. The renovation includes repairing damaged areas, installing new shelves, updating furniture, and adding computers for students who need digital access.

What will you do with the funds? (₱15,000 Budget)

• ₱5,000 — New bookshelves and storage units
• ₱4,000 — LED lighting upgrade for reading areas
• ₱3,000 — Tables, chairs, and study desks
• ₱2,000 — Two refurbished computers for student use
• ₱1,000 — Paint, repairs, and general improvements

Where will it be located?

The renovation will take place at the community library in **Barangay San Mateo**, serving students, readers, and families from surrounding areas.
